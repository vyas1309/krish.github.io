CORE IDEA

Uplynk is reimagining education by transforming exam-centric classrooms into launchpads for creators, entrepreneurs, and innovators. Using satellite-powered, solar-enabled, and offline-first technology—complemented by online access—we deliver modular learning kits focused on real-world skills like AI, coding, blockchain, finance, leadership, and emotional intelligence.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with proven pedagogical methods to create engaging learning experiences that prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

The Problem

India's education system still emphasizes rote learning, ignoring emotional growth, creative thinking, and practical life skills. Students lack exposure to entrepreneurship, innovation, and modern technologies.

EdTech solutions remain internet-dependent, leaving rural and underserved areas behind. Even in cities, parents often seek external programs to fill these gaps in traditional education.

Target Audience

  • Primary Customers: Private and government schools (urban + rural), government bodies, and NGOs seeking scalable education reform.
  • End Users: Students (Grades 6-12) needing future-facing education, and teachers requiring modern training and support.

Our programs are designed to be accessible to all students, regardless of their background or location.

Revenue Model

  1. School Subscriptions - B2B annual packages with content, broadcasts, and teacher bootcamps.
  2. Government & NGO Partnerships: Setting up solar-powered learning hubs and training infrastructure.
  3. Teacher Certification Programs - Satellite and online-based premium training and mentorship.
  4. Online Learning Modules – Optional add-ons for schools and individuals to access content through digital platforms.

Our Vision

To make world-class education accessible everywhere—online and offline—empowering every learner, regardless of geography, to unlock their full potential and shape the future.

We believe in creating equal opportunities for all students to develop the skills they need to thrive in the 21st century economy.
